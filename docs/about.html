Welcome to the Bangalore Chapter of Tezpur University Alumni Association!

The Bangalore Chapter of Tezpur University Alumni Association is a non-profit association that tries to encourage and support a mutual helpful correlation between the alumni and the newcomers along with the University as well.

Our Mission:

As we have a big presence in Bengaluru-India's high-tech industry, to provide superior marketplace career opportunities with assistance of alumni through different volunteering or invited sessions on soft and technical skills programs that develop the student knowledge is our exclusive mission. We are dedicated to cultivating a strong bond between the alumni, students and the University as well.

Our Vision:

Tezpur University is a well-recognized international organization that is recognized as the top 100 in universities in Asia. With a joint effort by the committed Board of Directors and the Alumni network, to offer a platform with different career-centric programs that will foster the life-long relationship between the campus and its Alumni is our vision.

Our Guiding Force:

  • Raising fund to organize a constructive and helpful the career-centric skill development training.
  • Maintaining a database of Alumni with their skills and expertise for recommendations.
  • Building a strong association with Alumnus via different career fair, seminars, career growth training sessions, and soft skills development training.
  • Creating a platform to share the success stories along with helpful guidance.
  • Organizing different functional and helpful training sessions for the members.
  • Diversity and Leadership Excellence with Life-long Learning.
  • Maintaining a connection and involvement with the University community.
