Memorandum of Understanding - Tezpur University Alumni Association – Bangalore Chapter

As per the Registration under societies Registration Act XI of 1860 –

Name of the Body:

  • The name of the society - Tezpur University Alumni Association – Bangalore Chapter which shall be known as the Association consequently.

Objectives of Tezpur University Alumni Association – Bangalore Chapter:

  • 1. To maintain an Alumni data sheet with experience.
  • 2. To improve the technical and managerial skills via different sessions.
  • 3. To aid alumni for an all round development.
  • 4. To encourage the friendly relations among all the members of the Association and the alumni as well via seminars, career fair, training and development programs etc.
  • 5. Promote Career guidance, interactive sessions and collaboration to know about all the prospects.
  • 6. To serve the common cause of the member’s general interest.
