By-Laws of Tezpur University Alumni Association – Bangalore Chapter

Definition:

These By-Laws along with the rules and regulations shall be read along with the Memorandum.

Name of the Body:

  • The name of the society - Tezpur University Alumni Association – Bangalore Chapter which shall be known as the Association consequently.
Registered Office:

No.5F, Arati Adora, Chikka, Belandur, Bangalore, Karnataka – 560035

Objectives:
  • To encourage the friendly relations among all the members of the Association and the alumni as well via seminars, career fair, training and development programs etc.
  • Promote Career guidance, interactive sessions and collaboration to know about all the prospects.
  • To serve the common cause of the member’s general interest.
  • To maintain an Alumni data sheet with experience.
  • To improve the technical and managerial skills via different sessions.
  • To aid alumni for an all round development.
Our Encouragers:
  • Tezpur University Alumni Association – Bangalore Chapter may have patrons approved by its Governing Body.
Our Ad-Hoc Committee:
  • With the advisory committee, an ad-hoc committee shall be constituted in the absence of the executive committee.
Special Invitee:

Executive Committee of the Association is authorized to induct eminent members / honorary members of the Association as social invitees as and when considered necessary.

Honorary Members:

An eminent person from the industry can be given an honorary member of the association in the Annual General Body Meeting (AGM).

Our Closure:
  • By three-fourth (3/4) vote of the members in a Special General Body Meeting (SGBM) convened for this purpose with at least twenty-one (21) days’ prior notice given to each member and will be allowed to vote by proxy.
  • In the occasion of termination of the Society, the assets remaining on the date of dissolution shall under no circumstances be distributed among the members or the executive committee members. The same shall be transferred to parent society.

We provide alumni association, alumni network and encourages alumni engagement.
